March 2026 has been one of the busiest months in AI history. Here's a digest of the most notable developments this week.

## Major Model Releases

- **GPT-5.4 (OpenAI)** — Released March 5. Features a 1.05M token context window, three variants (Standard, Thinking, Pro), and a new Tool Search architecture. Scored 75% on OSWorld-V, above the 72.4% human baseline. Source: [buildfastwithai.com](https://www.buildfastwithai.com/blogs/ai-models-march-2026-releases)

- **Qwen 3.5 Small Series (Alibaba)** — Ranges from 0.8B to 9B parameters, designed to run on laptops or mobile phones. The 9B variant scored 81.7 on GPQA Diamond, beating OpenAI's gpt-oss-120B. Source: [llm-stats.com](https://llm-stats.com/ai-news)

- **DeepSeek V4** — Launched around March 3. Claims 1 trillion total parameters with only 32B active per token — fewer active parameters than V3 despite being vastly larger. Source: [devflokers.com](https://www.devflokers.com/blog/ai-news-march-24-2026-releases-breakthroughs)

- **MiniMax M2.7** — Available via the MiniMax Agent and API as of March 19. Targets software engineering, office productivity, and research workflows with autonomous debugging capabilities. Source: [humai.blog](https://www.humai.blog/ai-news-trends-march-2026-complete-monthly-digest/)

- **NVIDIA Nemotron 3 Super** — Announced at GTC. A 120B-parameter enterprise coding model scoring 60.47% on SWE-Bench Verified. Source: [mean.ceo](https://blog.mean.ceo/new-ai-model-releases-news-march-2026/)

## Video & Image AI

- **LTX 2.3 (Lightricks)** — 22B-parameter Diffusion Transformer released in early March. Generates synchronized video and audio in a single pass, supports up to 4K at 50 FPS for clips up to 20 seconds. Source: [buildfastwithai.com](https://www.buildfastwithai.com/blogs/ai-models-march-2026-releases)

- **Helios (ByteDance + Peking University + Canva)** — Generates full 60-second videos at real-time speed on a single GPU. Source: [humai.blog](https://www.humai.blog/ai-news-trends-march-2026-complete-monthly-digest/)

- **MAI-Image-2 (Microsoft)** — A new text-to-image model from Microsoft's superintelligence team, with improved lighting, realism, and text rendering. Being integrated into Microsoft products with developer API access. Source: [marketingprofs.com](https://www.marketingprofs.com/opinions/2026/54448/ai-update-march-20-2026-ai-news-and-views-from-the-past-week)

- **Firefly Custom Models (Adobe)** — Now in public beta. Lets users train image generators on their own brand assets for consistent style and character output. Source: [niftytechfinds.com](https://niftytechfinds.com/ai-2026-roundup-february-march/)

## New Use Cases & Products

- **Siri Overhaul (Apple)** — Apple is overhauling Siri with Google's Gemini model (1.2T parameters) running on Private Cloud Compute. Targets cross-app awareness and on-screen context. Tied to iOS 26.4 in March 2026. Source: [crescendo.ai](https://www.crescendo.ai/news/latest-ai-news-and-updates)

- **Manus Desktop App** — Manus launched a local desktop agent that can interact with files, apps, and workflows directly on a user's machine, beyond cloud-based environments. Source: [humai.blog](https://www.humai.blog/ai-news-trends-march-2026-complete-monthly-digest/)

## Trending GitHub Repos

- **[OpenClaw](https://github.com/topics/ai-2026)** — Open-source personal AI assistant running on local hardware, integrating with WhatsApp, Slack, Discord, and more. Surpassed 210,000 stars. Note: Cisco disclosed supply chain and prompt injection vulnerabilities. Source: [buildmvpfast.com](https://www.buildmvpfast.com/blog/best-open-source-ai-projects-github-2026)

- **[n8n](https://github.com/topics/ai)** — Workflow automation platform with native LangChain and LLM integration. Crossed 150k stars. Source: [bytebytego.com](https://blog.bytebytego.com/p/top-ai-github-repositories-in-2026)

- **[Ollama](https://github.com/topics/ai)** — Local LLM runner written in Go. Passed 162k stars. Source: [bytebytego.com](https://blog.bytebytego.com/p/top-ai-github-repositories-in-2026)

- **[Dify](https://github.com/topics/ai)** — Agentic workflow development platform with RAG, MCP, and orchestration support. Hit 130k stars. Source: [medium.com](https://medium.com/the-pythonworld/top-12-github-ai-repositories-that-are-actually-worth-your-time-in-2026-ca4afe2bfa8b)

- **[Shannon](https://github.com/topics/ai-2026)** — Autonomous AI pentesting tool that executes exploits and collects evidence, not just reporting vulnerabilities. Gained 21,665 stars in one month. Source: [dev.to](https://dev.to/ji_ai/what-100-trending-github-projects-tell-us-about-where-ai-is-actually-going-4977)

## Industry Context

- OpenAI surpassed **$25 billion** in annualized revenue and is reportedly considering a public listing in late 2026. Anthropic is approaching **$19 billion** in annualized revenue. Source: [crescendo.ai](https://www.crescendo.ai/news/latest-ai-news-and-updates)

- The open-weight vs. proprietary gap has narrowed from years to months. Efficiency and edge-deployability are increasingly the differentiators, not raw parameter count.
